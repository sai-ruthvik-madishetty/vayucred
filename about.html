About Us

Digital MRV & Carbon Credit
Platform for India

VayuCred is an India-first digital MRV and carbon credit aggregation platform that transforms small solar farms and EV fleets into verified, registry-ready revenue streams.

We exist for the asset owners the carbon market has historically ignored — enabling them to earn from carbon credits without complexity, high costs, or technical barriers.

Built for India's
Underserved Clean Energy Assets

Across India, thousands of 1–10 MW solar farms and distributed EV fleets are already reducing emissions. Yet most never earn carbon revenue.
